## Root Cause
ICU Message Format specification does NOT support:
- Arithmetic expressions inside placeholders
- Mathematical operations (+, -, *, /)
- Non-ASCII variable names (only in message text)

Valid:   {count} records
Invalid: {total - count} records
Invalid: {-remaining} records
Invalid: {HÃ¶he} cm
